🍎 Get Your Widget

Set up in less than 1 minute!
No downloads, no imports.

⏱️ Takes 60 seconds • 6 simple taps
1Open Shortcuts app
Find the blue "Shortcuts" app on your iPhone and open it.
2Tap + (Create New)
Tap the + icon in the top right corner.
3Add "URL" Action
In the search bar at bottom, type "URL"
Tap the "URL" action to add it.

Then paste this into the URL field:
https://gustavoibenc.github.io/intermezzos/intermezzos.json
(Tap and hold the URL above → Copy)
4Add "Dictionary" Action
Search for "Dictionary"
Tap "Get Dictionary Value"

In the Key field, type:
delivered[0].fact
5Add "Show Result"
Search for "Show"
Tap "Show Result" to add it.
6Name & Add to Home
Tap ⋯ (three dots) at top
Type name: Intermezzo Fact
Tap Share icon (square with arrow)
Scroll down → Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Tap Add in top right

✨ DONE! Widget appears on your home screen!
💡 Pro Tip: After adding, long-press the widget to change its icon color to match your wallpaper!
